Transport from Bangkok International Airport to your hotel in Bangkok for your first night’s stay. (Please note, for free airport pick up you must meet us at 2.00 pm on your selected start date). This excludes Camp Phuket; at this location we will meet you in Phuket airport at 2:00pm.​

To all Camp locations, included activities and English Camp placements.​


Accommodation

During your first week of Camp we will take you on an elephant conservation visit.​
